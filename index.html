Our Mission

Not just a grow blog.

Cannabis Farm started as a growing guide — and it still is one, at its core. But cannabis is bigger than the grow tent. It's medicine, science, history, and culture. Our goal is to be the place you come back to no matter which side of the plant you're curious about, with honest, clearly explained information and no fluff.
